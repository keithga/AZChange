Healthcare

Healthcare costs are crushing Arizona families. Prescription drugs, insurance premiums, emergency room bills, and hospital costs keep rising, while Republicans in Washington continue pushing cuts that threaten healthcare access for working families, seniors, and veterans. Independent voters know healthcare shouldn’t be a luxury.

We need NEW LEADERSHIP to support lowering prescription drug prices, protecting coverage for pre-existing conditions, expanding access to rural healthcare, and stopping insurance companies from putting profits ahead of patients. Arizona families deserve affordable care they can actually use when they need it.

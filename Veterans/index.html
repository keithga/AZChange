Veterans

Arizona is home to thousands of veterans who served this country with honor, yet too many still struggle to access healthcare, housing, mental health services, and earned benefits. Veterans deserve more than speeches during election season, they deserve action.

We need NEW LEADERSHIP to fully fund veteran healthcare, reduce wait times at the VA, expand mental health support, tackle veteran homelessness, and ensure benefits are protected for the people who sacrificed for our country. Supporting veterans should never be a partisan issue; it’s a matter of basic respect and responsibility.

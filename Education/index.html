Education

Arizona families want great schools, safe classrooms, and graduates who are prepared for good-paying jobs. But after years of Republican control, Arizona continues to rank near the bottom nationally in teacher pay, school funding, and student outcomes. Parents are tired of overcrowded classrooms, constant teacher shortages, and politicians treating public education like an afterthought.

We need NEW LEADERSHIP! So that every child, whether they live in Phoenix, Tucson, Yuma, Flagstaff, or rural Arizona, deserves a quality education and a real opportunity to succeed. Investing in teachers, career training, and neighborhood schools is how we build a stronger economy and a stronger Arizona.

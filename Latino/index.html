Latino

Arizona’s Latino communities are deeply woven into the state’s culture, economy, and future. Most Arizonans agree the immigration system is broken, but chaos, fear, and political theater are not solutions. Families, businesses, and law enforcement all need a system that is secure, fair, and humane. Independent voters understand we can enforce the law without tearing communities apart or treating every immigrant like a threat.

We need NEW LEADERSHIP to support strong border security combined with practical immigration reform that targets criminals and traffickers while respecting hardworking families who contribute to Arizona every day.

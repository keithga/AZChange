Prices

Trump promised lower prices “on Day One,” but families are still paying too much at the grocery store, at the gas pump, and for basic necessities. Working people don’t care about political slogans, they care whether they can afford rent, groceries, and school supplies. Republicans talk constantly about the economy, but too often their policies favor giant corporations while everyday families struggle to keep up.

We need NEW LEADERSHIP to lower costs by cracking down on price gouging, increasing competition, strengthening supply chains, and investing in American jobs so Arizona workers can finally get ahead instead of falling behind.

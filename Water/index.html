Water

In Arizona, water is life.
Yet Republican leaders have repeatedly failed to protect one of our most precious resources. While Arizona families worry about drought and long-term water security, politicians allowed foreign interests, including controversial Saudi owned operations pumping massive amounts of groundwater to take advantage of weak oversight.

We need NEW LEADERSHIP to ensure Arizona water protects Arizona communities first, with stronger groundwater protections, smarter conservation policies, and long-term planning that keeps our farms, cities, and economy sustainable for future generations.
